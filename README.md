R6-Nova-X offers several settings to change how you play the game. Understanding these features helps you use the software effectively.

### ESP and Wallhack
ESP stands for Extra Sensory Perception. This feature draws information about your enemies directly onto your screen. You see boxes around opponents, their names, and their health status. Wallhack allows you to see these markers even when players move behind walls or solid objects. This assists you in planning your movement through the map.

### Aimbot
The Aimbot automatically adjusts your crosshair toward enemy targets. You can adjust the smoothness and speed of this movement. Set your preferred key for activation to ensure you only use the assist when you intend to fire.

### No Recoil
Weapons in Rainbow Six Siege have recoil patterns that move your camera while you fire. The No Recoil function removes this kick. Your shots land in a straight line, which improves your accuracy during long-range fights.

### Triggerbot
The Triggerbot monitors your crosshair. When an enemy enters your sights, the tool fires your weapon instantly. This minimizes the time between spotting a target and landing a hit.
